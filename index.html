Features and Services

Explore everything we have to offer.

Our partner stores include advanced, user-friendly self-checkout systems designed to enhance the shopping experience. These stores utilize smart shelves capable of detecting weight fluctuations, which signal when items are either removed from or placed on the shelves. The smart shelves work in conjunction with our smart carts, which feature integrated RFID sensors to identify the products currently in the cart. Each product is labeled with an RFID price tag to enable this functionality. Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) systems at all checkout areas and exit points, ensuring a secure and efficient security framework.
Our stores include self-checkout kiosks, where customers can easily scan, pay, and proceed with their purchases. Cards are distributed at store entrances: non-members are issued temporary cards after verifying their identification, while members receive permanent cards linked to their accounts. These cards are also used to unlock the smart carts.

Mission & Vision

Guiding Principles and Aspirations

Mission

Our mission is to bring innovative solutions and technology in the field of Grocery Shopping in the Philippines, starting with enhancing the Filipino shopping experience. We aim to establish a more efficient, modern and user-friendly shopping environment for every Filipino while boosting the local economy businesses

Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize the shopping system in the Philippines, by becoming a provider of innovative technology. And personalized shopping experience that sets a new standard for retail excellence in the country. We strive to build a future where every Filipino enjoys a seamless, modern, and fast shopping experience. Our vision not only extends beyond just technology but also strengthening communities through accessible, user-friendly, and inclusive shopping environments.
